# 英会話 重要単語・フレーズまとめ

## DL6-1 Comparing Culture & Goods and Services

**daintily**：上品に、お淑やかに

例）Women tend to eat daintily.

**to vs for（前置詞の使い分け）**：to は「場所」、for は「行動・目的」

例）The restaurant I went to (場所) / The restaurant I went for lunch (目的)

**established**：知られている、評判が良い、定評のある

例）I wouldn't want to take a chance with a new brand.（定評のあるブランドの方が安心）

**tempted**：(〜したい)気持ちにさせられる、惹かれる

例）I was tempted to buy the new model.

**become famous**：有名になる

※ "become to be famous" は誤り。正しくは "becomes famous"

## D6-8 Discussing

**rock bottom**：どん底、これ以上悪くなれない状態

例）Things have hit rock bottom.

**fad**：一時的な流行、短期間のブーム

例）That hairstyle was just a fad in the 80s.

**mullet**：マレットヘア（前は短く、後ろは長い髪型）

例）He's rocking a mullet these days.

例）He's on a carnivore diet — only meat.

※ Carnival /カーニヴァル/（ブラジルのカーニバル）と発音注意

**enrichment**：(動物園の)動物のための遊具・刺激物

例）The zoo added new enrichment toys for the monkeys.

**seaweed**：海藻

例）Seaweed is a common ingredient in Japanese cuisine.

**I'd like to get a physical answer**：直接会って答えが欲しい（physical = 対面の）

例）Ask me about hiking next time I see you — I'd like to get a physical answer.

## DL6-6 Discussing Etiquette

**dream destination**：夢の目的地、憧れの場所

例）My dream destination is Kyoto.

**young adulthood**：若い成人期、青年期

例）In my young adulthood, I enjoyed riding motorbikes on winding roads.

**up to / by**：〜まで（期間表現）

例）Generally, we say Happy New Year up to the 5th. / By the 5th, we stop saying HNY.

**reluctance**：気が進まないこと、ためらい

例）She showed reluctance to join the team.

**stroller**：ベビーカー

例）She pushed the stroller through the park.

**horsing around**：ふざけ回ること、騒ぐこと

例）Stop horsing around in class.

**pet peeve**：個人的にイライラする習慣・癖

例）My pet peeve is people chewing loudly.

**wouldn't experience this situation**：この状況に遭遇しないだろう（"couldn't meet this situation" は不自然）

例）I wouldn't experience this situation in Japan.

**Live and let live**：自分も生き、他人も生かせ（互いに干渉せず生きよう）

例）My motto is "live and let live."

**miscalculated**：計算違いをした、見誤った

例）I miscalculated the time.

**originated from**：〜に由来する（"origined from" は誤り）

例）This tradition originated from China.

**Thank you for humoring me**：付き合ってくれてありがとう（= 注意を向けてくれてありがとう）

例）Thank you for humoring me on this topic.

**high tolerance for stress**：ストレス耐性が高い

例）I am easily stressed but I have a high tolerance for stress.

**mindset**：考え方、心構え

例）He has a positive mindset.

**take stress out on (something)**：〜にストレスをぶつける

例）I take my stress out on my tasks.

**coping mechanism**：対処メカニズム、ストレス対処法

例）Exercise is my coping mechanism.

**reduces conflict**：対立を減らす

例）A pro is that it reduces conflict between my colleagues and I.

**remains unaware**：気づかないままである

例）A con is that she remains unaware of the effect of her actions on me.

## Discussions O7 Unit 6 - Discussing Etiquettes

**oddball**：変わり者、風変わりな（やや否定的だが丁寧な言い方）

※ weird (悪い) → oddball (悪いが丁寧) → unique (ポジティブ)

例）Japan is the oddball / unique one.

**ancestors**：祖先（祖父母、曾祖父母など）

例）My ancestors came from Kyoto.

**gather**：集まる、収集する

例）We gather for New Year.

**individualist** /インディヴィジュアリスト/：個人主義者

**collective**：集団主義の、全体の（社会を個人より優先する）

例）Japan has a collective culture.

**inherit**：相続する、受け継ぐ（親から得る）

例）I inherited this watch from my grandfather.

**will**：遺言書（署名済みの法的文書）

例）He left everything to his children in his will.

**spouse** /スパウス/：配偶者（夫または妻の法的呼称）

例）Please bring your spouse to the event.

**by default**：デフォルトで、自動的に（遺言が不要な場合）

例）By default, the spouse inherits everything.

**diploma**：卒業証書

例）I have a diploma in computer science.

**good on paper**：書類上は良い（実際は別という含み）

例）He's good on paper. / It's good on paper but not in the real world.
